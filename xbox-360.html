Xbox 360

The Xbox 360 was the 2nd console from Microsoft release about 4 years later than the Xbox original, it was revealed at this exact date of May 12, 2005, then later released at the exact date of November 22, 2005. Its major competitors are the sonny play station 3 , and the Nintendo Wii. the Xbox 360 came with many new features including multimedia features, such as YouTube, Netflix, and other cool stuff like that, and also access to music. You could also play demos and play games online, but of course you have to pay a certain fee to have all these available features to you. One of the biggest issues with the Xbox 360 is that it had a very common catostraufic failure, also known as the red ring of death, once you see it, your Xbox either has a small issue that is relatively easy to fix, or it is very difficult to fix, and it basecly means that your Xbox 360 is broken and very hard to fix. The Xbox 360 was still however a very good selling console, selling millions over the course of years, but it was still not the highest selling competing console at that time, but while still significantly strengthening the Xboxes popularity among consumers and making it a great competitor among other gaming consoles during that time, and what also strengthened it was the exclusive games that came with the Xbox 360, such as halo or project scarlet Wich are all very fun games.
