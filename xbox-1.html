Xbox one

The Xbox One is the 3rd video game console developed by Xbox Game Studios. Announced in May 2012, it is the successor of the Xbox 360 and the second base console in the Xbox line of video game consoles. Its release date is set for November 22, 2013, in North America, and November 24, 2013, in PAL regions. It is the first Xbox console to be released in mainland China, specifically in the Beijing Free Trade Zone. Microsoft marketed the console as an "all-digital" entertainment system, even though it is not technically all digital... hence the name "Xbox". It features an AMD Accelerated Processor Unit (APU) based on the x86-64 architecture. The Xbox One's controller was designed over the Xbox 360's controller, with a redesigned body and triggers capable of delivering directionally sensitive haptic feedback. The system places an increased emphasis on online gaming, as well as social media features and the ability to stream game footage to other platforms. Games can be played locally through a LAN connection on supported Windows 10 devices and streamed to other consoles through the same connection. The console includes a redesigned Kinect sensor, which offers improved motion tracking and voice recognition capabilities. The console supports Blu-Ray discs, and allows users to view live TV channels through an optional digital tuner. The system optionally includes a redesigned version of Microsoft's Kinect camera, which provides improved facial recognition and gesture detection. So over all the Xbox one was a relatively successful console, but yet again it did not sell as good as its competitors.
