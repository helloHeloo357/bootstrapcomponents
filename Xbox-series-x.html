Xbox series X

The Xbox Series X will be the next generation of the Xbox series, following the Xbox 1, following with the Xbox One S. It will feature an upgraded processor, faster RAM, and a new custom-designed GPU. It will also be Microsoft's most powerful console. The Xbox Series X runs on Windows 10X, an updated version of Windows 10 that Microsoft unveiled back in June 2019. The OS features a redesigned user interface, a revamped game store and a brand new gaming environment called in Wich it is called Project XCloud. The Xbox Series X plays both the existing Xbox One titles as well as PC games and it does this through streaming technology. It also offers better voice recognition capabilities and Cortana integrations. The console was launch on November 7, 2019 and it was announced during E3 in 2019. The Xbox series X is similar to the Xbox series S, but it does have some very notable changes, the Xbox series s is white while the Xbox series x is black, the Xbox series x comes with a higher storage ability and it is also much more powerful than the Xbox series s, capable o f 4k gaming, while the highest the Xbox series s can do is 1440p. The controllers are very good that come with it, not as good as the ps5 controller though as the ps5 controller comes with many more features compared to the new Xbox controller, such as haptic feedback, trigger stuff, and it just over all looks better than the new Xbox controllers.
